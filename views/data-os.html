DataOS

DataOS is an open framework and toolkit for creating data flows
to collect, inspect, process and publish data. You can fully automate your
data collection, processing and publication with the DataOS.

Why use an open framework?

No proprietary tooling and no vendor lock-in

Easily extensible for adding custom functionality

All outputs and artifacts are standard-based and fully portable
